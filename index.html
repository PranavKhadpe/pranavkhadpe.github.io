I am a first year PhD student in the Human-Computer Interaction Institute at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am co-advised by Chinmay Kulkarni and Geoff Kaufman.

I am interested in building tools that help people online help each other.

I did my undergrad at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur where I spent 5 amazing years working towards a masters degree in Electrical Engineering. During my undergrad years, I interned with the Stanford HCI Group where I worked with Michael Bernstein and Ranjay Krishna and I also spent a summer at Microsoft Research India where I worked with Monojit Choudhury and others in the Multilingual Systems group.

The best way to reach me is pkhadpe [at] cs.cmu.edu. Feel free to reach out if you have questions about my work, would like to collaborate, or just want to brainstorm together!
